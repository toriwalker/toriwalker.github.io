Why my dad sends me a new song every day while I'm away at college

My dad used – and continues to use – music as a gateway to communication and relationship-defining. I grew up riding next to him in his red 2011 Subaru Outback to school every morning, swaying my hips in my seat to Grateful Dead’s “Shakedown Street.” My dad infused his jukebox judgement and taste in funky, alternative tunes into me early on. Little did I know at the time, as he inserted various CDs into the mysterious dashboard slot, that he was just trying to talk to me. That was his way of forming a bond, father to daughter.
